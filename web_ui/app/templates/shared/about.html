About ML-EASY

Our Mission

ML-EASY aims to make machine learning accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

We believe that artificial intelligence and machine learning should be tools that anyone can use to solve problems and create innovative solutions. Our platform simplifies the process of testing and training machine learning models for image classification, making it easy for beginners to get started and for experts to iterate quickly.

Key Features

  • Simple Testing Interface: Upload images and get instant classification results
  • Multiple Model Architectures: Choose from various pre-built neural network architectures
  • Easy Dataset Management: Create, upload, and manage your training datasets
  • Fine-tuning Capabilities: Adapt existing models to your specific use cases
  • Web Scraping Tools: Automatically collect images for your datasets
